THREE-POINT REFORM PROTOCOL

:: Deploying systematic justice upgrades ::

01

EDUCATION_PROTOCOL

First-Offense Warning Program

Replace financial penalties for first-time minor violations with educational warnings. Students complete the "Gaucho Parking Primer" to transform their violation into a learning opportunity, breaking the punishment cycle.

OUTCOME: Education over punishment

TRANSFORM_PROTOCOL

Convert violation.penalty → knowledge.upgrade

02

PAYMENT_SYSTEM

Accessible Payment Plans

Expand the payment plan program to better comply with California law. Deploy an online portal, extend application windows to 120 days, and proactively inform students of available financial assistance for all, not just indigent persons.

COMPLIANCE: CA Vehicle Code 40220

ACCESSIBILITY_PROTOCOL

Deploy financial.relief when needed.most

03

JUSTICE_PROTOCOL

Fair Appeals Process

Eliminate the requirement to pre-pay fines before contesting them at higher levels. Establish a student-majority Parking Appeals Committee to ensure peer review and restore true due process.

FEATURE: Student-majority review

DUE_PROCESS

Justice != bankAccount.balance
