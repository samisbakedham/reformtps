A Three-Point Plan for Reform

Practical, proven, and student-focused solutions.

01

Education First

Replace first-time minor violation fines with a mandatory online "Gaucho Parking Primer" module. This approach prioritizes education over punishment, reducing accidental re-offenses and financial strain.

02

Financial Access

Implement a simple, online payment plan portal compliant with CA Vehicle Code 40220. This ensures that students facing financial hardship have accessible, clear options to resolve fines without incurring further penalties.

03

Fair Appeals

Abolish the "pay-to-appeal" requirement that creates a barrier to justice. Establish a student-majority Parking Appeals Committee to ensure impartial, peer-led reviews of all contested citations.
