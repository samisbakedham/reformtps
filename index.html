Three-Point Reform Protocol

Practical upgrades for a fairer system.

01

Education Protocol

First-Offense Warning Program

Replace penalties for first-time minor violations with educational warnings and a "Gaucho Parking Primer."

Education over punishment
02

Payment System

Accessible Payment Plans

Overhaul plans to comply with CA law, with online portal and 120-day windows.

CA Vehicle Code 40220 Compliance
03

Justice Protocol

Fair Appeals Process

No pre-payment for contests; student-majority appeals committee.

Student-majority review
